**We're here to help.** Whether you have a technical issue, billing question, or need guidance on using Bookify effectively, our support team responds quickly and clearly.

## How to Reach Us

### Email Support

**support@bookify.it**

Best for:
- Technical issues with book generation
- Billing and credit questions
- Account management requests
- Feature requests and feedback
- Partnership inquiries

**Response time:**
- General inquiries: Within 24 hours (Monday-Friday)
- Critical issues (payment errors, generation failures): Within 4 hours
- Enterprise inquiries: Within 12 hours

When emailing, please include:
- Your account email
- Detailed description of your issue or question
- Screenshots if relevant (especially for technical issues)
- Book ID if related to a specific generation

## Response Time Expectations

We're a small, focused team committed to excellent support. Here's what to expect:

**Business Hours:** Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm CET (Central European Time)

**Response Targets:**
- **Critical issues** (can't access account, payment errors, generation failures): 4 hours
- **General support** (how-to questions, guidance, non-urgent issues): 24 hours
- **Feature requests and feedback:** 24-48 hours
- **Enterprise inquiries:** 12 hours

**Weekends:** Limited support availability. Critical issues are monitored, but non-urgent inquiries receive responses on Monday.

## Enterprise & Team Inquiries

Looking for team accounts, volume licensing, or custom integrations?

**Email:** enterprise@bookify.it

Include:
- Company name and size
- Estimated monthly book volume
- Team size needing access
- Any specific requirements (SSO, custom branding, API access)
- Preferred contact method and time zone

We'll schedule a call to discuss your needs and provide custom pricing.
